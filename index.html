Pudding

A companion-style focus app that helps students stay motivated without relying on punishment-based productivity tools.

Many students struggle to stay focused in distraction-heavy digital environments. Pudding reframes studying as a more supportive and emotionally engaging experience through an anime-inspired virtual companion, lightweight progress feedback, and a friendly focus flow designed for students who feel stressed by traditional study apps.

Project Overview

Pudding was developed through a full design-thinking process, from needfinding and synthesis to ideation, prototyping, usability testing, and high-fidelity refinement.

Problem

Students often find it difficult to maintain attention while studying. Existing productivity tools typically rely on strict timers, blocking systems, or pressure-heavy workflows that can feel punitive and emotionally draining.

Solution

Pudding explores an alternative model: a virtual companion that motivates users with encouragement, emotional presence, and lightweight progress cues so that studying feels more supportive and less isolating.

Core User Tasks

These tasks connect the final interface back to the user needs identified through interviews and later design evaluation.

Easy

Start a Focus Session

Users begin a session quickly with minimal setup. This is the most important entry point because friction at the start makes people abandon productivity tools.

Medium

Stay Motivated During Study

Users receive encouragement and visual feedback from the companion. This supports attention without relying on punishment or overly rigid controls.

Medium

Complete a Session

Users finish a study session and review simple progress feedback. This reinforces positive study habits and gives closure to the workflow.
